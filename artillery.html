Core Features Across All Games

  • Artillery/Trajectory Physics: Aim projectiles using angle & power; sometimes affected by wind & gravity.
  • Destructible Terrain: Explosions modify the battlefield for strategic depth.
  • Upgrades / Weapons: Unlock new projectiles, powers, and unit types.
  • Stickman / Mini Stick Figures: Characters are simple but animated, often customizable.
  • Game Modes: Single-player, PvP multiplayer, survival/challenge, score attack.
  • Controls: Mouse, keyboard, or touch mini-stick on mobile.
  • Visuals & Audio: 2D animations, particle effects, dynamic backgrounds, sound effects.
  • Progression / Rewards: Levels, achievements, in-game currency, unlockables.
  • HTML5 Compatibility: Works in modern browsers across platforms.
  • Save & Multiplayer: Local storage for progress; online battles on select games.
